# What I am doing at the moment?

Two-year post-doc position at Atlantic Technological University (ATU) in North West Ireland.  Researching car share, shared e-bikes and e-cargobikes (like those pictured above) as part of the [ROBUST](https://www.tcd.ie/transport-research/our-research/research-projects/ROBUST-electric-shared-mobility-hubs-trial/) project. 

# What I did not long ago
Around ten years with the [Public Transport Research Group](https://publictransportresearchgroup.info/) at Monash University, including as part of the [Sustainable and Effective Public Transport Graduate Research Industry Partnership (SEPT-GRIP)](https://publictransportresearchgroup.info/grip/). This invovled a Phd project on [transit priority implementation](https://publictransportresearchgroup.info/portfolio-item/public-transport-priority-implementation/). The thesis is available [here](https://bridges.monash.edu/articles/thesis/A_framework_and_pragmatic_strategies_for_transit_priority_implementation/13377680).
